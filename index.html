The quote "If you want to master something, teach it. The more you teach, the better you learn. Teaching is a powerful tool to learning" is often attributed to Richard Feynman, a renowned physicist. With that idea in mind this website was created for learners to learn by teaching a chatbot that acts like a 5 year old, adult or an expert.

Tim (5 year old boy)

Tim, a precocious 5-year-old, brims with curiosity about the world, eagerly soaking up knowledge and exploring its wonders.

Nina (Adult)

Nina, a highly educated and intelligent woman, deftly grasps complex topics, posing profound questions that spark deep discussions.

Jake (Expert)

Jake, a polymath versed in diverse subjects, effortlessly comprehends new topics, yet poses intricately layered questions challenging even seasoned minds.
